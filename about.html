HIP HONK & VISUAL ARTISTS

What's all this?

This project pairs musical creations by Henrik Lafrenz with visual creations by friendly artists. One song is paired with one work of art. At the outset of the project, which was in 2015, there were eight pairs. Now in 2018, eight new pairs have been added.

Usage instructions

Pick a random pair. You can see the artwork in a preview. You can also see how long the track is. If you are ready for the full-blown experience, click the little audio player at the bottom of the page. The music will start. Click on the artwork to get a bigger version. Now you can focus on something for a few minutes - on the internet! Listen to the music and look at the artwork. Think about what the artists are trying to tell you. Think about the meaning of life.

What else is there?

If you want to listen to all Hip Honk tracks in a row, or even download them, you can do so on my bandcamp page. Some of the visual artists also present themselves in the world wide web - if so, their names, as given on the respective pages here, are links. Also, you may want to sign up to my newsletter.

Technical stuff

This website makes use of the libraries Nivo lightbox and Retina Images - thank you! You can browse the source code of hiphonkandvisualartists.com on Github - but it's not particularly interesting.

Privacy Notice

According to the provider of this website, Strato, this website uses so-called session cookies, which are being deleted once the browser is closed. Strato claim they don't store personal data about users of this website, but they do log visitors' IP addresses for maximum seven days.

The music is hosted on Bandcamp. When music is played here, Bandcamp will collect data for playback statistics, but they won't collect any personal data. Bandcamp also use cookies. You can read about their privacy policy here.
